The iPhone

Home

A phone, an intenet brower, an iPod

Launched in 2007, the iPhone combined a phone, an iPod, and an Internet browser in one device. With its multitouch screen and later the App Store, it completely changed how people communicate, listen to music, and use apps.

The iPad

A bigger iPhone without phone

Introduced in 2010, the iPad created a new category between smartphones and laptops. Its large touchscreen made it easy to read, watch, create, and play, becoming both a tool for entertainment and work.

The Mac

Apple's line of personal computers, known for their sleek design and powerful performance.

The Mac began with the Macintosh in 1984, the first widely popular computer with a graphical interface and mouse. Over time, it evolved into laptops and desktops known for their elegant design, reliability, and macOS, widely used by creatives, students, and professionals.
